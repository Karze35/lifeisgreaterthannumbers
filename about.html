karson utzinger

My name is Karson Utzinger, and I am an aspiring storyteller. I’ve been telling stories through film and music since I was little and hope to continue this into my old age. I have a lot of ideas but do recognize the limitations of even the good ones. This is why I’m in college, studying film and music at Chapman University attempting to learn a bit about how to produce these ideas. The hope of my work is that it shines light into the day to day promoting the good.

The mission "Life>Numbers" represents a step away from our metric- driven world. While it may be a crucial aspect of our daily living, I do believe that there's room to breathe, separate from quantification.

If you have any interest in helping me out on this journey, please do let me know!

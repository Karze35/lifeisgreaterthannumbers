My Timeless Creator

My Timeless Creator. The story begins with an entity whose name cannot be pronounced. His form neither grand nor timid. His sense is that of one, He is all, therefore all is in Him, there is no separating my Creator from Himself. I call Him God, My Timeless Creator. In the beginning my Timeless Creator thought His first thought, and it was the thought of Love. Love was His first creation. Within His Love He knew that to be loved He would have to create Lovers. God is good, God is with me

Are You with me? Mom says so, but are You here? Dad doesn’t talk about you much, but he prays at dinner. Are you closer to some then to others? They tell me to look for You in everybody, but they also tell me to stay away from some. I don’t understand. The other day I listened to someone who doesn’t believe in You, and I really liked what He was saying. I saw You in Him. So why doesn’t He see You? Why don’t I always see You at church?

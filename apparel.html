here we hope to implement our mission of "life > numbers" into humanitarian effors. by purchasing a product, you will be helping to fill the gap between life and numbers for children hoping to explore the arts.

Our first mission will be held in the city of detroit. this shirt has been designed by a native artist and will be sold at a price of $35.

$15 of every shirt sold will be donated to the DPSCD (Detroit Public School Community District) fund for arts and culture.

$20 of every shirt will go back to Life > Numbers to cover the cost of production.
